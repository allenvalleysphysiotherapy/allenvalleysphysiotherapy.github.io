About Me

I trained at Newcastle School of Physiotherapy, (at the old Polytechnique as it was then) and qualified as a Chartered Physiotherapist in 1983.

My career began in the NHS, working as a physio in hosiptal wards and physiotherapy departments for several years.

I began working in private practice in 1990, running a busy, city centre clinic for 19 years. I am now based solely at the Allen Valleys clinic

Joyce Charlton

Professional Memberships

I am member of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy, registered with the Health Professionals Council, PhysioFirst and the Association of McTimoney Corley Spinal Therapists.

Book an appointment

To make an appointment, telephone the clinic on 01434 618423 or email info@allenvalleysphysiotherapy.co.uk

If I am unable to answer, please leave a message and I will return your call.

It is not necessary to have a GP referral, although if you are using health insurance you usually need a GP referral letter. Please check with your insurance company.

I am listed as a treatment provider for all the major health insurance companies such as PPP, BUPA, etc.
